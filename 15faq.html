Favourite asked Questions

Can you care for autistic child?

Child Ease provides safe and nurturing childcare services to all children, including those with special needs, such as autism. Our staff members are trained to provide individualized care and support to children with various developmental needs.

Is there a registration fee?

Child Ease does not mention any registration fee on its website. It is advisable to contact them directly for any inquiries about fees.

Do we need to prepare meals for our child?

Child Ease provides healthy meals and snacks to children throughout the day. Parents are not required to prepare meals for their children.

What happens when my child becomes ill at the childcare?

In case a child becomes ill at Child Ease, the childcare facility has a policy that requires the child to be isolated from other children and the parent or guardian to be notified immediately.

Can we apply for newborns?

Child Ease provides childcare services for children of various ages, including newborns. It is advisable to contact the facility directly for information on age requirements and availability.
