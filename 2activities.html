Our programmes

Nursery

1 years old - 2 years old

The early years of a child's life are crucial for their overall development, and early childhood care plays a vital role in shaping their future. This is why the Child Ease curriculum is designed to offer a comprehensive approach to learning and development for young children. Our goal is to provide a welcoming and nurturing environment that fosters growth and encourages children to explore and learn at their own pace.

At Child Ease, we understand that each child is unique and has their own strengths and weaknesses. Therefore, we take a personalized approach to learning, tailoring our curriculum to the needs and interests of each child. Our team of experienced and dedicated educators work closely with parents to ensure that each child receives the individual attention they need to thrive.

We believe that a well-rounded education is essential for a child's growth and development. Therefore, our curriculum includes a range of activities, including academic subjects, arts and crafts, music, sports, and more. By providing a variety of learning opportunities, we aim to stimulate the child's mind, body, and creativity, helping them develop into well-balanced individuals.

Kindergarten

3 years old - 5 years old

Kindergarten is a critical stage in a child's development, as it serves as the foundation for future learning. At our center, we believe in providing a nurturing and engaging environment for young children to learn and grow. Our kindergarten curriculum is designed to support children's social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development. We use a play-based approach that allows children to learn through exploration and discovery, while also developing essential skills such as language, literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving.

Our kindergarten teachers are experienced and dedicated professionals who understand the unique needs of young children. They provide individualized attention to each child, ensuring that they receive the support and guidance they need to reach their full potential. We also recognize the importance of parental involvement in a child's learning journey, and we encourage parents to be actively involved in their child's education.

At our center, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our kindergarten students. We maintain a clean and hygienic environment, and we follow strict safety protocols to ensure that children are always safe and secure. Our kindergarten classrooms are equipped with age-appropriate learning materials, toys, and books that are specifically designed to engage young learners.

Preschool

6 years old

At our center, we firmly believe that a child's early years are the foundation for their lifelong learning journey. That's why we have merged the National Standard Preschool Curriculum (KSPK) with a play-based approach to create a unique curriculum that promotes both cognitive and emotional development. Our approach is child-centered, and we aim to provide a safe and stimulating environment where children can explore and learn at their own pace.

One of our primary goals is to teach children empathy. We believe that empathy is an essential life skill that helps children build meaningful relationships with others and navigate the world around them. Through our curriculum, we encourage children to identify and understand their own emotions and those of others, to develop a sense of compassion and respect for diversity, and to learn how to communicate effectively.

At our center, we also recognize that every child is unique and has their own strengths, interests, and learning styles. Therefore, our curriculum is designed to be flexible and adaptable, catering to the individual needs of each child. We encourage children to explore their interests and passions, providing opportunities for them to engage in a range of activities such as art, music, sports, and outdoor play.

Elementary

7 years old - 12 years old

At our center, we recognize the importance of providing after-school care that is both dependable and enriching for children. Our after-school program is specifically designed for children between the ages of 7 and 12, providing a safe and nurturing environment for them to learn and grow outside of regular school hours. With the help of our friendly and supportive staff, parents can have peace of mind knowing that their children are in good hands.

One of the unique aspects of our after-school program is our focus on developing soft skills in children. In addition to providing academic support, we also offer activities that help children develop skills such as critical thinking, public speaking, and respect for elders. We believe that these skills are essential for children to succeed in life, both academically and socially.

At our after-school program, we believe in the importance of developing soft skills in children. We encourage critical thinking through puzzles, brain teasers, and group discussions, which help children become independent learners who can analyze information and make informed decisions. We provide opportunities for public speaking through activities such as debates, presentations, and storytelling, which help children build confidence and develop their communication skills.
